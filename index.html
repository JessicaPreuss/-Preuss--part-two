Your Virtual Pet

Welcome to Your Virtual Pet! On this website, you can name your pet and track your pet's happiness and energy levels by logging the care you give your pet choosing from the options feed, pet, or walk.

STEP 1. First, decide on a pet name. Enter your pet's name when prompted

STEP 2. Once you've decided on a name and logged it above, start logging the care you give your pet. When prompted, enter input feed, pet, or walk
